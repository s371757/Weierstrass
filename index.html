About Our Dear Weierstrass

He was a hedgehog. We are mathematicians. Every day we saw him while driving home from the office.

He was brutally murdered on 31.05.2025, too soon, by a car on the road. We will revenge him.

Rest Peacefully, Little One

Though your quills are now still and your snuffles silent, your spirit remains bright in our memories. Thank you for the quiet companionship, the unconditional love, and the countless moments of joy you brought into our lives. You were more than just a pet; you were family.

"How lucky I am to have something that makes saying goodbye so hard." - A.A. Milne (Winnie the Pooh)

We will miss you terribly, sweet Weierstrass. Run free and explore endless fields of dream-clover.

We will miss you for multiple eternities, your Magabogo and the rest of the team.
